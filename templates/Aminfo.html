Apply to become an Auxr ambassador

Applications

Applications to become an Auxr ambassador are accepted on a rolling basis.

Why join the AAP team?

By joining the AAP team, you join a community of Auxr lovers that get exclusive look at new Auxr updates. The Auxr Ambassador Program (AAP) is a community full of music lovers that are all things music discoveryand collaboration.

Benefits

Free premium features to make your group listens better and free merch, including hats, T-shirts, and phone cases.

What is an Auxr Ambassador?

An Auxr Ambassador is a student in a highschool or college that goes around there campus as a promoter of Auxr. They tend to be very outgoing and spirited individuals that desire to share their love of music.

How to apply

It's as easy as 1, 2, 3!

1. See if you are eligible

Auxr hires college and high school students from all over the country who are eager to share their love for music.

2. Start Your Application Now!

Start your application with one simple click.

3. What Happens Next?

Give us 1 to 2 weeks to review your application, and you will receive an email regarding your application status!
